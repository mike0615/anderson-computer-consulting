## Who We Are

Anderson Computer Consulting (ACC) is a Florida-based technology consulting firm founded by Mike Anderson — a retired U.S. Navy Chief Information Systems Technician with over 30 years of hands-on IT experience.

We specialize in enterprise network engineering, cybersecurity, managed IT, and government technology consulting. Every solution we deliver is air-gapped capable, automation-driven, and production-ready.

## Our Philosophy

We don't sell products — we build solutions. Every engagement starts with understanding your environment, your constraints, and your mission. Then we design and deliver infrastructure that actually works, can be handed to your team, and documented properly.

> "Enterprise-grade infrastructure doesn't have to mean enterprise-grade complexity. It means getting it right."

## Our Approach

| Principle | What It Means |
|---|---|
| Air-Gapped First | Every design works fully offline |
| Open Source | No vendor lock-in, no license surprises |
| Automation Driven | Ansible-first, GitLab CI/CD, reproducible builds |
| Documentation Heavy | Stakeholder-ready docs delivered with every project |
| Production Ready | Solutions tested in real environments, not just labs |

## Our Background

- **ICD 503** security framework operations

- **Daily stack:** FreeIPA, Cisco, Juniper, XCP-ng, KVM, Ansible, GitLab, Graylog, Wazuh, LibreNMS
